Wait... What even is Tomodachi life?

I'm glad you ask, dear viewer.

Nintendo's Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream is a 2026 remake of the 2013 Tomodachi Life, which was an international remake of the 2009 Tomodachi Collection.

All games are focused on creating "Mii's", a style of character that debuted on the Wii in 2006, and having them build relationships with each other. Both remakes of the game added more customisation and things for your Mii's to do, with TL:LtD topping out with complete island customisation, facepaint, original items, and a "woke adgenda", finally allowing your Mii's to have pronouns and dating preferences.
